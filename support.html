✦ Step-by-Step Guides

How to Resolve Common Concerns

Guide 01 Received the wrong order

1
Stay calm and check your receipt

Confirm what you ordered against the receipt given at the counter before approaching the crew.

2
Approach our crew at the counter

Show your receipt and the incorrect item. Our staff will verify the discrepancy right away.

3
Wait for the correction

We will prepare your correct order as soon as possible and prioritize it in our queue.

Guide 02 Submitting an inquiry or feedback online

1
Choose your contact channel

Decide whether to email us, message us on Facebook, or call/text. See the Contact Channels section above.

2
Provide complete details

Include your name, the date and time of your visit (if applicable), and a clear description of your concern or question.

3
Wait for our response

We aim to respond within 24 hours on business days. Facebook Messenger typically gets the fastest reply during café hours.

Guide 03 Loyalty card stamp not applied

1
Present your card before ordering

Always show your loyalty card to the crew before paying so stamps can be applied immediately.

2
Check your receipt

If a stamp was missed, present both your receipt and your loyalty card to our crew within the same visit.

3
Contact us if you've already left

Message us on Facebook or email us with a photo of your receipt. We will verify and credit your stamp accordingly.
