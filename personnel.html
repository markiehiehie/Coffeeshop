👤 Ownership

Founder & Owner
Jovee Sison

Jovee Sison is the founder and driving force behind Waffee Café. With a background in small business management and a deep passion for food and community, he envisioned a café that felt like home — affordable, warm, and family-run. He oversees all major business decisions, financial management, and long-term strategy, ensuring that Waffee stays true to its roots while continuing to grow.
