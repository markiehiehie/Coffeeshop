Lights, Camera, Latte! Eat Bulaga Goes Live at Waffee Café

April 10, 2024 will forever hold a special place in Waffee Café's story. The beloved noontime variety show Eat Bulaga chose our cozy coffee shop as their live broadcast venue — and the energy inside was absolutely electric. Cameras rolled, the audience cheered, and our crew made sure every guest was caffeinated and comfortable throughout the entire production.

"We never imagined a national TV show would set up their cameras right here in our little café — but that day proved that Waffee is more than just coffee. It's a community."
— Waffee Café Team

From prepping the venue at the crack of dawn to serving drinks to the production team and live audience, every member of the Waffee crew was at their best. The experience reminded us why we do what we do — bringing people together over great food and an even greater atmosphere. Our signature waffles and drinks were featured on-air, giving us a moment in the national spotlight that we are beyond grateful for. Salamat, Eat Bulaga!

Honored at City Hall: Waffee Café Receives Local Excellence Award

It was a proud moment for everyone in the Waffee family when our city government recognized us with a Local Business Excellence Award at a formal ceremony held at the City Hall. The award honored our contribution to the local community — from providing a safe and welcoming space for students and workers, to supporting neighborhood events and giving livelihood to local staff. Receiving the plaque from our city officials was a humbling reminder that every cup we serve matters far beyond our four walls. We are deeply grateful to our loyal customers whose support made this recognition possible. This award belongs to every one of you.

A Day of Giving: Free Snacks for Every Customer

In the spirit of bayanihan, Waffee Café organized a special charity day where we gave away free snacks to all our customers — no purchase necessary. From golden waffles to warm pastries, everything was on the house for one beautiful afternoon. The smiles on our customers' faces, especially the students and families who came in, made every moment worth it. It was our way of saying "salamat" — thank you for choosing us, for trusting us, and for being part of the Waffee community. This was just the beginning; we look forward to more giveback moments in the future.
