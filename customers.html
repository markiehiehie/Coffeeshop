Who We Serve

Waffee Café serves individuals looking for a comfortable and affordable place to relax, work, or study at night. Whether you're cramming for an exam, wrapping up a project, or just craving something warm — you're always welcome here.

Market Demographics

Waffee Café primarily serves individuals in the 15–35 age bracket residing in or near Malabon City, Metro Manila. Our customer base is made up of people who are active during nighttime hours — whether by choice or by necessity — and who value comfort, affordability, and atmosphere.
