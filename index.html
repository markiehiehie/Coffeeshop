Welcome to Waffee Café

Waffee Café is a family-owned night café serving coffee, waffles, pastries, fries, and daily rice meals from 5:00 PM to 12:00 MN. Born from passion and built on family, we are Quezon City's go-to late-night comfort spot.

Who We Are

Waffee Café is a small family-run food and beverage establishment operating as a night café in Quezon City, Philippines. We specialize in handcrafted coffee drinks, freshly made waffles, crispy fries, pastries, and rotating daily rice meals — all prepared in-house by the family team.

What started as a home-based pre-order business in 2018 grew through years of bazaars and community events before opening its first physical location in July 2023. The café operates as a cozy, welcoming space designed for students, workers, freelancers, and anyone looking for quality food at a fair price late into the night.
