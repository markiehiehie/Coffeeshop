Our Story

About Waffee

From a pre-order food business to a full café — driven by family and passion.

Our History

2018

Waffee Café started as a small pre-order food business, run by a tight-knit family with big dreams and a love for great food.

2018 – 2023

The family joined local bazaars and food fairs across the Metro, slowly building a loyal community of customers who kept coming back for more.

July 2023

They opened their first physical café — a cozy night spot in Quezon City, serving from 5 PM to midnight. A dream, finally made real.

2024

Waffee Café received a Local Business Excellence Award from the city government, recognizing its contribution to the community and local livelihood.

Family Business

The café is owned by Jovee Sison and operated by Gavin Ineco, Mae Jimenez, and Calvin Ineco, who manage both the coffee bar and kitchen. Every cup and every plate is a team effort—made with pride and served with heart.

Nature of Operations

The Waffee Café is a food and beverage retail business operating as a night café. We prepare and serve handcrafted coffee, waffles, pastries, fries, and rotating daily rice meals directly to walk-in customers from our Muzon, Malabon City location.

The Story Behind the Waffle

"The waffles are their merienda, made using a family recipe passed down from their grandmother, which they later turned into a business. The batter is cooked in a waffle press until golden and crisp on the outside, while staying soft and fluffy inside. Customers can enjoy it with different toppings like syrups, ice cream, or savory add-ons, served warm right after cooking for a homemade, comforting feel."

— The Sison-Ineco Family

Long before there was a café, there was a kitchen experiment. Back in 2018, the family began making waffles at home — not as a business, but simply because they loved them. What started as a weekend treat quickly became a household staple, with neighbors and friends asking for extras.

Realizing they had something special on their hands, the family decided to bring their homemade waffles to local bazaars and food fairs. The response was immediate — lines formed, batches sold out, and regulars kept returning. The waffle wasn't just a product; it became the heart of the brand.

Today, the waffle remains Waffee Café's signature — still made with the same care and love as that very first batch in the family kitchen. It's crisp on the outside, fluffy on the inside, and served with toppings that have been perfected over years of community feedback. Every waffle tells the story of where we came from.
